Welcome to Nemo!

You're starting on the home page, where you can find your swipe stack.

These are other people's profiles, which you can choose to like or pass on.

To like someone, double tap the right side of their profile.

To pass on someone, double tap the left side of their profile.

You can find out more about someone by tapping on the banner with their name.

Just tap on the banner again to close the info section.

To change the profiles appearing on your swipe stack, change your nemosearch.

You can get to it by clicking on the magnifying glass.

It's all up to you now,

good luck!

Tutorials for the other tabs will show up when you go to them.
