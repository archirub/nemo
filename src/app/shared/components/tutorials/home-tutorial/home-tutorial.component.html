Welcome to Nemo!

You're starting on the home page, where you can find other profiles in your catch-stack.

To like someone, double tap the right side of their profile.

To pass on someone, double tap the left side of their profile.

You can find out more about someone by tapping on the banner that displays their name.

To collapse the profile, simply tap on the banner again.

If you want to change the profiles in your catch stack, simply tap the search icon to enter nemo search.

You can refine your search for similar interests, societies, and much more!
