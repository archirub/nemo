This is your profile page!

Here, you can edit the details about you that others see.

Under the view tab, you can see your profile exactly as others will see it.

You can switch between editing and viewing your profile with the toggle in the top corner.

If you have more than one picture on your profile, you can drag the icons around to switch their order.

The top three will appear first, followed by the bottom three.

When you start editing, the save and cancel buttons will appear.

You won't be able to view your profile again until you save or cancel all of your changes.

Some details about your profile cannot be changed.

Other details - for example your gender identity - can be changed under your settings.
